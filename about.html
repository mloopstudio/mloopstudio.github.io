About Mloop Studio

A remote team united by purpose and passion

Who We Are

Mloop Studio is a collective of creative professionals who believe in the transformative power of storytelling. We are animators, writers, designers, and dreamers who share a common vision: to create content that nurtures the emotional and intellectual growth of children.

Working remotely from different corners of the world, we've built a studio culture that values flexibility, creativity, and meaningful collaboration. Our team members bring diverse perspectives and experiences, enriching the stories we tell and the characters we create.

Our Approach

We believe that children's entertainment should do more than just entertain. Every project we undertake is crafted with intention, designed to help young viewers develop emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and a passion for learning.

Our animation style is warm and inviting, with attention to detail that respects our young audience's intelligence. We tackle complex themes in age-appropriate ways, creating stories that children can grow with and return to as they mature.

Our Values

Intentionality

Every story element serves a purpose in supporting children's emotional and cognitive development.

Authenticity

We create genuine, relatable characters and situations that reflect real childhood experiences.

Inclusivity

Our stories celebrate diversity and help children understand different perspectives and experiences.

Quality

We're committed to excellence in every frame, ensuring our animation meets the highest standards.

Working Remote, Dreaming Together

As a remote studio, we've proven that physical distance doesn't diminish creative collaboration. Our distributed team model allows us to work with the best talent regardless of location, bringing together diverse voices and perspectives that enrich our storytelling.

We leverage modern tools and communication platforms to stay connected, share ideas, and build projects collaboratively. This flexibility enables our team members to work in environments where they're most creative and productive, while maintaining the close collaboration essential to great animation.
