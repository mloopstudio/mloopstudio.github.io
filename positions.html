By Mloop Studio Human Resources Management

December 2025

1. Who We Are

Mloop Studio is an independent animation studio dedicated to creating thoughtful, emotionally meaningful children’s entertainment. Through hand-drawn 2D animation and interactive educational media, we aim to support curiosity, creativity, and self-awareness in young audiences.

Our team works remotely across different time zones, connected by a shared purpose rather than a shared location. We value kindness, clear communication, and collaboration rooted in trust.

3. How to Apply

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis via our website application form. Please include relevant work samples or portfolios. Whether we move forward or not, you'll get an email reply within 7 days maximum. No ghosting, we promise!

4. Working Together

All roles are remote and open internationally. Our team uses Discord for quick chats, Google Drive for file sharing, and clear project guidelines. All team members are hired as Independent Contractors (freelancers). We primarily use PayPal or Wise. Mloop Studio covers the sender’s transaction fees; the contractor is responsible for their local bank fees. Payments are processed within 7–10 business days of receiving a valid invoice.
